Welcome!

Hi, I'm Sarah Davis!

I have been a research associate in the Hurley Lab at Indiana University since November, 2017 and work on social experience and seroternergic modulaiton of the inferior coliculus in mice. I am most excited about my job when I am solving problems and love coming up with creative solutions within interdiciplinary environments.
I'm applying to Ph.D. programs this fall and exploring new opportunites to grow as a scientist and work on neuroprosthetic devices to improve the independence of humans affected by neurological or physical limitations.
In my free time I enjoy gardening, creating with anything I can get my hands on, and expanding my kinestic abilities though group exercise.
