Welcome!

Hi, I'm Sarah Davis!

I have been a research associate in the Hurley Lab at Indiana University since November, 2017 and work on social experience and serotonergic modulation of the inferior colliculus in mice. I am most excited about my job when I am helping others achieve their goals and finding creative solutions to complex problems.
I'm currently applying to clinical research positions and exploring new opportunities to grow as a professional facilitating research using my interpersonal and creative problem solving skills.
In my free time I enjoy gardening, creating with anything I can get my hands on, and expanding my kinesthetic abilities though group exercise.
